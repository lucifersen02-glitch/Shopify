## üìê Image Specifications

- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD) or larger

- **File Size**: Under 2MB (for fast loading)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (landscape)

## üöÄ How to Add Images to Your Shopify Theme

1. **Download** the images to your computer
2. **Go to Shopify Admin** ‚Üí Online Store ‚Üí Themes ‚Üí Customize
3. **Click** on "Hero Carousel" section
4. **Upload** images:
   - Click "Slide 1 Image" ‚Üí Upload your first image
   - Click "Slide 2 Image" ‚Üí Upload your second image
   - Click "Slide 3 Image" ‚Üí Upload your third image
5. **Save** your changes

## üí° Pro Tips

- Choose images with **bold colors** or **high contrast** for better text visibility
- **Avoid** images with too much detail in the center (where text appears)
- **Test** how images look with the text overlay
- Use **consistent style** across all 3 slides for a cohesive look
